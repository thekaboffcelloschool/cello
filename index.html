What We Offer

A Full Musical Education

Suzuki Method

Specialized lessons for beginners ages 3.5–7. Parent involvement, loving encouragement, and graded repertoire build confident young musicians from the first lesson.

Competition Prep

Students regularly earn top prizes in local, regional, and national competitions including Carnegie Hall debut recitals through the American Protégé program.

Cello Choir

The Bow Lightlys ensemble performs at Carnegie Hall, the Embassy of France, charity events, and senior living centers — bringing music to the community.

Teaching Materials

Original progressive repertoire volumes, scale books, and annotated Bach Suites freely available for students and colleagues to download.
