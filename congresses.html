National & International

Cello Congresses

World Cello Congress III (2000)

John Kaboff and the Bow Lightlys were featured guest artists at the World Cello Congress III, where they performed an internationally broadcast cello ensemble workshop with Yo-Yo Ma. Mr. Kaboff also adjudicated the preliminary competition rounds at the Master Class Competition.

Sixth American Cello Congress (2001)

The Bow Lightlys performed a full cello choir concert as part of Mr. Kaboff's guest lecturer appearance at the Sixth American Cello Congress, where he also delivered his lecture "Creating a Clear Pulse for Student Ensembles."
