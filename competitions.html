Student Achievements

Competitions & Youth Orchestras

  • Washington Performing Arts Society Competition

    Students have taken top prizes at all levels, most recently sweeping all categories in the 2016 competition.

  • American Protégé International Competition

    Prize winners perform debut recitals at Carnegie Hall's Weill Recital Hall. Multiple Kaboff students have performed there in 2015, 2016, 2017, 2022, 2024, and returning April 2026.

  • Concert Artists International Competition

    In 2018, a 6-year-old student performed at Weill Recital Hall in Carnegie Hall as a prize winner.

  • Progressive Musicians Music Competition

    The Bow Lightlys were 2022 Laureates, performing at Carnegie Hall's Weill Recital Hall.

  • YoungArts Competition

    Colin H. was a 2020 YoungArts Competition Winner.

  • Tennessee Cello Workshop Competition

    Genevieve B. took 1st place in multiple categories at the 2018 competition (Kol Nidrei and Orbit by Philip Glass).

  • Capital Symphonic Youth Orchestra

    Students participate in chamber music and full orchestra performances. In 2019, ensembles coached by Mr. Kaboff appeared at the Kennedy Center Millennium Stage and on ABC7 Washington.

  • Washington International Competition for Strings

    John Kaboff served as chairman of this competition in 2022, and previously adjudicated in 2006 and 2012.
