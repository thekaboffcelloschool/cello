Masterclasses & Residencies

John Kaboff Lectures

As a frequent guest lecturer, John Kaboff has conducted master classes and performed at institutions across the United States, Australia, and England, including:

  • Flinders Street Conservatorium, Adelaide, Australia
  • Purcell School of Music, London, England
  • Indiana University Summer String Program
  • Michigan State University
  • Longy School of Music, Boston
  • The Music Academy of North Carolina
  • University of Texas (Rio Grande Valley)
  • University of Tennessee & University of Rhode Island
  • Appalachian State University — Artist in Residence (2011, 2019)
  • Tennessee Cello Workshop — Featured Artist (2010, 2014, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025)
  • Suzuki Association of Australia — Workshops in Melbourne & Adelaide
